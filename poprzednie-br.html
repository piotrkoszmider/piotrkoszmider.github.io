Ratajczyk (introduction to mathematics), K. Sieklucki, W. Marciszewski (topology), Cz. Bessaga, J. Mogilski (funcional analysis), K. Krzyzewski (ODE), R. Sztencel (probability), J. Krempa (algebra) A. Bialnicki-Birula(differential equations) B. Bojarski (analytic functions, PDE) S. Nowak (algebraic topology), W. Guzicki, T.Bartoszynski, A.Krawczyk (set theory), W. Guzicki,, W. Just (combinatorics), , W. Kowalczyk (logic) W. Kowalczyk (computational complexity) A. Skowron (programming for medicine) W. Szlenk (retarded differential equations).
