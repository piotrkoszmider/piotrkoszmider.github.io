  Warsaw University 1983 - 88
I studied direct M.A. programme in theoretical mathematics. Among my teachers were M. Misurewicz, M.Kuczma (analysis), R. Pol, M.Sobolewski (linear algebra), P.Zbierski, Z. Ratajczyk (introduction to mathematics), K. Sieklucki, W. Marciszewski (topology), Cz. Bessaga, J. Mogilski (funcional analysis), K. Krzyzewski (ODE), R. Sztencel (probability), J. Krempa (algebra) A. Bialnicki-Birula(differential equations) B. Bojarski (analytic functions, PDE) S. Nowak (algebraic topology), W. Guzicki, T.Bartoszynski, A.Krawczyk (set theory), W. Guzicki,, W. Just (combinatorics), , W. Kowalczyk (logic) W. Kowalczyk (computational complexity) A. Skowron (programming for medicine) W. Szlenk (retarded differential equations). I gainded Rector prizes for all academic years 83-88, the Minister of Higher Education Scholarship for 87-88, The third prize in Marcinkiewicz's competition for a mathematical paper by a student in 88.
